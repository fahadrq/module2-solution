Fruits

Fruits contain the necessary fibers that facilitate bowel function and facilitate digestion, thus helping to support the removal and disposal of waste. Protein

Protein is one of the basic components responsible for multiple functions in the body including building tissues, cells, and muscles. Protein with fat and carbohydrates is the main source of energy needed by the body to carry out vital processes. Protein consists of a group of twenty kinds of amino acids, of which nine are essential, that is to be obtained from protein-rich food sources, and other non-essential types that can be manufactured within the body.
